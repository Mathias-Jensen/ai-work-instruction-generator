# Cleaning Procedure for Inspection Camera Housing

## Metadata

Document title: Cleaning procedure for inspection camera housing  
Equipment or system: Vision inspection station  
Target user: Production operator  
Experience level: Beginner  

## Raw notes

Image quality was poor. Stopped machine at end of batch. Opened camera housing. Lens cover had dust and product residue. Wiped lens cover with approved cleaning cloth and cleaning liquid. Did not touch camera lens directly. Checked light ring for dirt. Cleaned inside of housing. Closed cover. Started machine. Ran inspection test with reference item. Image sharp again. Inspection passed.
